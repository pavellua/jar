INDUSTRIAL DOORS

FAST ACTION DOORS

The Dock Technik fast action door is ideal to help control climate and air flow in an area where there are high volumes of traffic. The opening/closing speed along with the correct activation and sequencing will ensure that the door is open for a minimal amount of time.

Our fast action door can be installed in a range of environments and we will select the exact model to meet your needs.

SECTIONAL DOORS

The Dock Technik sectional overhead door is very versatile with a wide range of sizes, track layouts and activation options available. With its high insulation performance, durability and safety features it is designed to provide reliable access over many years. Our door meets the EN 13241 1:2003 standard for quality and is supplied in a 40mm insulated panel as standard although 80mm is available for a higher level of insulation.

As our sectional overhead door is primarily installed on loading bays, we offer a range of integration options:

ROLLER SHUTTER DOORS

The Dock Technik roller shutter door comes in the insulated or un-insulated models. Our traditional un-insulated galvanized steel roller shutter suits a wide variety of applications, it provides a level or security in an opening that does not need to be opened and closed frequently and where climate control is not important.

Our insulated roller shutter also provides a good level of security but with its double skinned lath and polyurethane insulating core it offers protection in a temperature critical environment and also reduces noise pollution.

STEEL DOORS

At Dock Technik we offer a range of high-quality steel personnel doors and we can identify the best model to suit your needs. We have high security, internal personnel and emergency fire exit doors amongst other models and they come in industry standard sizes or can be tailored on request.

Our standard fire exit door has a 50mm thick door leaf with anti jemmy bolts on the hinge side and the frame itself is zinc plated sheet with a stainless steel, low profile threshold. There is a wide range of hardware and accessories available for the door to meet your requirements.
